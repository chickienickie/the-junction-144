The Restaurant

Welcome to The Junction 144, a fresh and exciting addition to Centre County, PA, founded in 2024. Nestled in the historic Davidson Inn building, where the Mountain Valley Diner once stood, we're thrilled to offer a new dining experience in this cherished location. At The Junction 144, we pride ourselves on delivering mouthwatering, high-quality meals in a warm, family-friendly setting.

Our mission is simple: to share delicious, straightforward food with our community.

Keep up with our weekly specials on Facebook, and join us soon to experience a meal that’s sure to delight. We can’t wait to welcome you!
